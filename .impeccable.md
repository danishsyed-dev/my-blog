## Design Context

### Users
- **Sarah (Hiring Manager / Recruiter)**: Evaluates portfolio depth and engineering quality. Context is quick scans (30s) and seeking a clean, professional candidate.
- **Dr. Patel (Research Collaborator)**: Seeks publications, venue accuracy, research areas, and academic rigor.
- **Alex (Mobile Browser)**: Recruiter or developer reading from LinkedIn on a phone.

### Brand Personality
- **Voice/Tone**: Professional, academic, authoritative, clean, and serious.
- **3-Word Personality**: Research-centric, Refined, Precision.
- **Emotional Goals**: Evoke confidence, intellectual depth, trust, and professional polish.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: Modern Academic / Editorial.
- **Color Palette**: Warm sand, off-black, slate gray, and a deep crimson/terracotta accent.
- **Theme**: Dual-theme support with a light/dark mode toggle. Dark mode features slate-tinted warm charcoal, light mode features warm paper/sand.
- **References**: High-end academic publications, minimalist typography layouts, monographic styles.
- **Anti-references**: Generic "AI slop" (neon blue/purple gradients, cyan-on-dark, glowing borders, card grids everywhere).

### Design Principles
1. **Typographic Contrast Over Borders**: Use sizes, weights, and serif-sans pairings (Playfair Display + Inter) for structure instead of boxing everything in cards.
2. **Harmonious Neutrals**: Avoid pure black (#000) or pure white (#fff). Warm-tinted off-blacks and sand-tinted light backgrounds.
3. **Intentional Accentuation**: Use the deep terracotta/crimson accent sparingly for interactive focus and semantic highlights, never decorative gradients.
4. **Fluid Motion & Accessibility**: Exponential ease curves (`cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)`) and strict keyboard/screen-reader accessibility (WCAG AA).
